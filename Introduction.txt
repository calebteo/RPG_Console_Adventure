----------------------------------------------------------------------------

You are a treasure hunter, who is searching for the lost treasure on an 
unexplored island. 
On the way to this Island, you were striked from an unknown source and your 
plane crashed. 
You wake up to the plains of the mountain jungle.
You must be quick and smart to find the treasure before you starve to death. 

----------------------------------------------------------------------------
Health = 0 => Game over 
Energy = 0 => Game over
Can't find Treasure => Game over

Find Treasure => Win

X = OUT OF BOUNDS
Y = YOUR CHARACTER
W = Wolf, O = Ogre
D = Dead Creature
C = TREASURE
